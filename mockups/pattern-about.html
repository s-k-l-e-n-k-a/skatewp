Our
approach

A documentary production house built on three convictions — depth, fluidity, and reach. We produce films that matter, with the people who live them.

01

Depth

We dive into people's realities

We produce with passion documentary films about real people. Diving deep into their issues and immersing ourselves in their realities — until the story reveals itself on its own terms, in their own words.

02

Fluidity

We swim through different currents

In form and content — sometimes upstream. Always combining a social perspective, creativity and quality images. We adapt to the story, not the other way around.

03

Reach

We move across uncharted waters

Working worldwide with an international network of filmmakers, artists and dreamers — assembled uniquely for every project. Borders are stories, not obstacles.
